# Product Overview

## What it is
A single-section "Hello World" landing page — the simplest possible web page: one clean, centered section with a greeting headline. Intentionally minimal, meant as a starting point that can grow into something more (waitlist, portfolio, promo page) later.

## Current scope
- **One section only.** A hero/greeting section with a "Hello World" headline and supporting line.
- No navigation, no footer, no extra pages.
- Static, no backend, no data persistence.

## Audience
The first visitors who land on the page — any general visitor arriving at the site. Today this is a clean foundation rather than a product aimed at a narrow user; positioning will sharpen once the page takes on a real purpose.

## Brand & tone
- **Tone:** clean, simple, friendly.
- **Visual direction:** minimal, lots of whitespace, centered content, one clear focal headline.
- Name/logo: not yet set.

## Strategic principles
- Start clean and simple; earn complexity only when there's a reason for it.
- Keep the single section sharp so it can later anchor a larger story (signup, showcase, or campaign).

## Status
Pre-build foundation. Defined as a minimal hello-world landing page; expect this overview to sharpen as the page gains a concrete purpose.
