# HelloPage
A clean, single-section "Hello World" landing page.
- Users: first-time visitors landing on the page; general audience.
- Core: one hero section with a clear title ("Hello World"), a short supporting line of text, and a single call-to-action button.
- Tone: friendly, minimal, modern.
- Scope discipline: ONLY one section. No nav bar clutter, no footer-heavy layout, no extra pages. Keep it a true MVP starting point.
- Anti-references: avoid busy multi-section marketing pages; avoid heavy imagery.
